Hydraulic vs Pneumatic Systems

Understanding the key differences to select the right technology.

Parameter Hydraulic Systems Pneumatic Systems
Working Medium Hydraulic Oil (Mineral / Synthetic) Compressed Air
Pressure Range Up to 350 bar Up to 10 bar
Force Output Very High (tons) Moderate (kN range)
Speed Moderate, highly controllable Fast, high cycle rates
Precision Excellent (incompressible fluid) Good (compressible air)
Cost Higher initial & maintenance Lower cost solution
Cleanliness Oil leaks possible Clean operation
Energy Efficiency Higher (90%+ pump efficiency) Lower (compressor losses)
Noise Level < 72 dB(A) Can be noisy (exhaust)
Safety Fire risk (oil), high pressure Safer (no fire risk)
Best For Heavy loads, precise positioning, presses Fast cycling, packaging, pick-and-place
Testiny Products Power Packs, Cylinders, Boosters Control Systems, FRL Units
