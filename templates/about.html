Our Story

Pioneering Industrial Solutions

Testiny Equipments Private Limited is a premier manufacturer and supplier of industrial machines, hydraulic systems, pneumatic solutions, and precision testing equipment. Established with a vision to deliver world-class engineering products, we have grown into a trusted partner for industries across India and beyond.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Chennai is equipped with CNC machines, precision measuring instruments, and a dedicated R&D center. Every product undergoes rigorous quality testing before delivery, ensuring compliance with international standards including ISO, API, ASME, and EN.

From concept design to commissioning, we provide complete turnkey solutions. Our team of experienced design engineers, PLC programmers, and project managers work closely with clients to deliver machines that exceed expectations in performance, reliability, and value.
